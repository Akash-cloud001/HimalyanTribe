duration: 4 nights / 5 days
starting point: delhi/chandigarh

The name Manali is derived from 'Manu-Alaya' which literally means 'the abode of Manu'. As the legend says, Manali was the place where Manu’s boat was found after the great floods and he stepped off his ark in Manali to recreate human life after a great flood had deluged the world. Old Manali village has an ancient temple dedicated to sage Manu. It is at an altitude of 6260 feet above sea level, Manali is a renowned hill station bestowing aesthetic beauty & mesmerizing natural appeal. Whether you are on a family trip or on honeymoon or just seeking a great time with close buddies. Himalayan tribe offers you the best KASOL KULLU MANIKARAN ROHTANG MANALI tour package that suits your budget, perfectly.
